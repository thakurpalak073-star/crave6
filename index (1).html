FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?+
Most deliveries arrive within 25-35 minutes depending on distance, restaurant prep time, and current demand. You can track your order in real-time.
Can I cancel my order?+
You can cancel your order within 2 minutes of placing it at no charge. After that, cancellation may incur a fee as the restaurant has already started preparing.
How do promo codes work?+
Enter a valid promo code in the cart page before checkout. Discounts are applied automatically. Each code has specific terms like minimum order value or cuisine restrictions.
Is my payment information secure?+
Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and are PCI-DSS compliant. We never store your full card details on our servers.
